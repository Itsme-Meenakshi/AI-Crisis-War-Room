# AI Crisis War Room: An Intelligent Crisis Management and Decision Support System

## Overview

Organizations frequently encounter unexpected crises such as cyberattacks, data breaches, product failures, service outages, and public relations incidents. During such situations, decision-makers must act quickly despite uncertainty, limited information, and significant pressure.

AI Crisis War Room is an AI-powered crisis management and decision-support platform designed to assist organizations in analyzing, understanding, and responding to critical events. The system leverages Generative AI to evaluate crisis scenarios, assess severity, identify affected stakeholders, analyze potential risks, and generate prioritized response strategies.

## Key Features

### Crisis Analysis

* Accepts natural language descriptions of crisis situations.
* Identifies crisis type and severity level.
* Evaluates immediate and long-term business impacts.

### Multi-Perspective Decision Support

The platform generates recommendations from multiple organizational perspectives:

* Business Strategy
* Public Relations
* Legal & Compliance
* Operations & Incident Response

### Risk Assessment

* Identifies potential operational, financial, legal, and reputational risks.
* Prioritizes risks based on impact and urgency.

### Stakeholder Impact Analysis

* Detects affected stakeholders including customers, employees, investors, partners, and regulatory bodies.
* Estimates stakeholder impact levels.

### Interactive Dashboard

* Crisis Severity Visualization
* Risk Heatmaps
* Stakeholder Impact Charts
* Recommended Action Timelines

### AI-Generated Response Plans

* Immediate response actions
* Short-term mitigation strategies
* Long-term recovery recommendations

## Technology Stack

* Frontend: React.js / Next.js
* Backend: Python (FastAPI/Flask)
* AI: OpenAI GPT Models / LLM APIs
* Database: PostgreSQL / MongoDB
* Visualization: Chart.js / Plotly
